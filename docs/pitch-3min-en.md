# Pitch: AI Governance in the "Agent-First" Era — 3 minutes

## 1. "The New Reality" (25 sec) — The Context

"We're living through a paradigm shift. Anthropic made it clear with Claude Code: the future of software development is agent-first. Humans direct, agents execute. This is no longer about autocomplete — today Claude writes entire features, refactors codebases, and opens pull requests autonomously. Companies are going to have hybrid workforces: humans and AI agents working side by side. But there's a critical problem..."

## 2. "The Blind Spot" (20 sec) — The Problem

"Companies are spending fortunes on Claude licenses and building autonomous agents. But ask any CTO today: are your agents actually increasing real team throughput, or just burning tokens and creating technical debt? They have no idea. They bought a Formula 1 engine, but they're trying to drive it with no dashboard."

## 3. "The Signal" (15 sec) — The Opportunity

"The irony is the data already exists. Claude Code emits standard telemetry via OpenTelemetry: cost, tokens, model used, success rate. But today, all that signal dies in each developer's terminal. Nobody is capturing it. Nobody is analyzing it."

## 4. "What We Built" (40 sec) — The Solution (Demo)

"That's why, inside Leanmote, we built **AI Governance**. The control panel for the agent-first era. It captures that telemetry and gives you complete visibility."

*(Show the live dashboard)*

- **Cost & Adoption:** "No more guessing. See exactly how much you're spending on Claude, who's using it, and the month-end forecast."
- **Efficiency:** "See the token ratio per task and which models — Opus, Sonnet, Haiku — are most efficient for each type of work."
- **Agent Workforce:** "See your autonomous agents as team members. How many tasks they completed, their cost per task, and their success rate."
- **AI Insights:** "And my favorite: Claude analyzing its own telemetry to give you actionable alerts. AI governing AI."

## 5. "The Magic" (20 sec) — The Difference

"The setup is almost embarrassingly simple. Zero friction. One single line of configuration in Claude Code and it starts reporting. No invasive agents installed on anyone's machine. We leverage the native telemetry Claude already generates — we just capture it and turn it into intelligence."

## 6. "Why It Matters" (15 sec) — The Real Value

"Anthropic is building the most capable agents in the world. But the engineering leader's job is not just to adopt agents — it's to govern them. Today a CTO can measure pull requests and cycle time in GitHub. Now, they need to govern the performance of their AI agents with the same operational rigor."

## 7. "The Vision" (15 sec) — The Close

"This is not a standalone tool. It lives inside Leanmote. The industry's next big move is crossing AI consumption with real Delivery data. Are the teams spending the most on Claude actually the ones shipping the fastest? That answer is worth millions — and we have it."
