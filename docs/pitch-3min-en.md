# Pitch: AI Governance in the "Agent-First" Era — 3 minutes

## 1. "The New Reality" (20 sec) — The Context

"We're living through a paradigm shift. The future of software development is agent-first. Humans direct, agents execute. Today AI tools write entire features, refactor codebases, and open pull requests autonomously. Companies are scaling AI fast across their entire engineering orgs. But there's a critical problem..."

## 2. "The Problem" (20 sec) — The Blind Spot

"Teams now use multiple tools — Claude, Codex, Cursor. Each has its own billing, its own data, its own silo. And no CTO can answer one simple question: is all this AI spend helping my business grow? They're buying Formula 1 engines — plural — but trying to race with no dashboard."

## 3. "The Gap" (15 sec) — The Opportunity

"Every tool reports cost, tokens, usage. But nobody puts it together. And nobody connects it to what actually matters: is AI helping my teams deliver real value to the business? All that signal exists. It's just invisible."

## 4. "What We Built" (45 sec) — The Solution (Demo)

"That's why, inside Leanmote, we built **AI Governance**. One single layer across your entire AI toolchain. It captures the telemetry every tool already emits and gives you complete visibility."

*(Show the live dashboard)*

- **Unified Spend:** "One view. Claude, Codex, Cursor — all in one place. Who uses what, how much it costs, and the trend."
- **Smart Comparison:** "Which tool works best for which task? Real data, not guessing. Stop paying for Opus when Haiku does the job."
- **Business Value:** "This is the one that changes everything. Team A uses more AI and delivers twice the business impact. Team B spends the same but nothing reaches customers. Now you know exactly where to look."
- **AI Insights:** "Claude analyzing its own telemetry — and every other tool's — to give you actionable alerts. AI governing AI."

## 5. "The Closed Loop" (20 sec) — The Difference

"But we don't just show you data. We apply harness engineering best practices and show you how each pattern affects your business outcomes. An agent keeps delivering code that gets rejected and reworked — tokens burned, time wasted, zero value. We detect that. And from that insight, you directly create a task for your harness agent to fix the workflow. Insight to action, all in one place."

## 6. "Why It Matters" (15 sec) — The Real Value

"Today a CTO can measure cycle time in GitHub and delivery in Jira. Now they can answer the question that matters most: is our AI investment actually accelerating our business? Scaling without governance is just scaling chaos."

## 7. "The Vision" (15 sec) — The Close

"This is not a standalone tool. It lives inside Leanmote — where AI usage meets real delivery data. We built the system that governs your AI tools, connects them to business value, and lets you deploy agents to close the gaps. **Observe. Understand. Act.**"
